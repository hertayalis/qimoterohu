Zach Rance comes out as bisexual; had a relationship with co-star Frankie Grande!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 5, 2024
  • Zach Rance, a Big Brother Season 16 contestant, has come out as bisexual.
  • He announced his sexuality during a recent interview on mental health.
  • He is dating his co-star Frankie Grande.

Frankie is the older brother of singer Ariana Grande. The two were on the show in the year 2014.

Zach Rance comes out as bisexual

The former reality TV star, Zach Rance was talking on the podcast With Love, Alexa. He was having a YouTube Conversation for The Mental Health Collection.

At that time, Zach confessed that he is bisexual now. He also revealed that he was straight all his life and had liked only women until now.

But when he appeared on the show Big Brother in 2014, things changed in his life. He got very close to Frankie Grande who is the big brother of American singer Ariana Grande.

Zach said:

“I fell in love with who he was as a person—super funny, super smart, you know, good-looking guy. And as time went on, we got so close that I wasn’t really sure if I had feelings for him or not,”

The couple’s relationship after the show

Frankie has yet not given any comments or inputs on what Zach has just revealed on the podcast. Zach also went on to say that after the show ended and everyone went home, his relationship with Frankie was more than that of a friend.

He continued:

“He was the first guy that I ever hooked up with. And after that night, I was very unsure about the direction of my sexuality because I like women parts,”

After this period in his life, he was uncertain of his sexuality. He did not know where to go from there. He added:

“I don’t want to say I tried—being gay, but I watched gay pornography and I was like, ‘Oh that guy, he’s good-looking, he’s hot,’ like would I want to hook up with him? And I’m like, no. Like, I wouldn’t want to make out with him,”

“So then, I was like, wait a second, is it just because Frankie is someone who I’m really close with?”

Zach and his realization

The reality TV star revealed that when he went for a photoshoot in New York City, he also hooked up with the photographer. He all the more got confused about his sexuality.

Zach said:

“But the more I thought about being with a guy, or like, you know, making out is one thing but doing more than making out with a guy is something I just don’t, I don’t want to do, and like, I’ve never tried it. But I just want to come out and say that I enjoyed hooking up with Frankie and the other guy I hooked up with. Clearly, I enjoyed it because things went down.”

The Big Brother alum concluded that he was bisexual with a leaning towards heterosexuality. He wanted to talk about it since he wants to inspire people to remain open-minded.

He feels that one can get attracted to a person’s mind or heart and hence have a sexual relationship with them.

Zach added:

“You don’t have to just be attracted to the way they look.”
