Mai Quynh's 10 Faves for Dark Lips

fair/light skin: Dolce & Gabbana Voluptuous Lipstick in Magnetic Monica

"This color ($33; saksfifthavenue.com)is not super dark-it is a red wine shade that has a deep red tone that looks gorgeous on fair to light skin."

light/medium skin: Givenchy Le Rouge Matte Lipstick in Violine Precieux

"This semi-matte shade ($36; sephora.com) leaves your lips in the perfect berry tone for light to medium skin."

medium/dark skin: NARS Semi Matte Lipstick in Scarlet Empress

"This color ($26; sephora.com) will create the perfect vamp on medium to dark skin without being too intense."

"This shade ($34; chanel.com) is very striking, but will look beautiful and not too dramatic on dark skin."
