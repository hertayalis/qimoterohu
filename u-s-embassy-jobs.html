How to Apply Online for U.S. Embassy Jobs in Pakistan?

  • To apply for this position click the “Submit Application” button. For more information on how to apply visit the Mission internet site at: (https://pk.usembassy.gov/embassy-consulates/jobs/current-vacancies/).
  • Include academic degree/ transcript/ detailed mark sheet and/or any other related document as a single attachment using tab “Other Document”.
  • Include professional and job related experience certificates/ letters of all previous work experiences and any proof (last drawn salary slip/appointment letter etc.) of current employment as a single attachment using tab “Other Document 2”.
  • Work/Residency Permit: Required, in-case you are not the citizen of host country.
  • Last date to apply online for U.S. Embassy Jobs in Pakistan is 25th June 2023.
