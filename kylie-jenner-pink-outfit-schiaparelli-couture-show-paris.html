Kylie Jenner Is a Pink Dream at the Schiaparelli Paris Couture Show

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 10, 2024

From beachy sets befitting the French Riviera to cozy dresses from her own Khy fashion line, Kylie Jenner has been treating this transition into summer as a time to show off her versatile style. For her latest look, she got pretty in pink to attend Schiaparelli’s Fall 2024 couture show today in Paris.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder was among the VIP names who showed up to see creative director Daniel Roseberry’s latest showing for the luxury fashion house. And she looked so stunning in one of the label’s sparkling designs: a monochromatic fit composed of baby-pink pieces that made Jenner look like a blinged-out peony.

courtesy of Kylie Jenner via Instagram//Instagram

More About Kylie

For its base, the look included a couple of intimates, including a corset-style bodysuit and knee-high stockings, which were layered under a semi-sheer strapless dress decorated with thousands of tiny gems. The gown featured a sweetheart neckline, while the bodice hugged Jenner’s body like a sparkly second skin.

BACKGRID

But while the dress was certainly showstopping, the accessories were the real scene-stealers. Up top, Jenner wore a thin netlike headpiece that completely wrapped around her head. As for her footwear, she matched the rest of her ensemble with a pair of satin pink pumps that looked like ballet shoes transformed for a night out on the town.

courtesy of Kylie Jenner via Instagram//Instagram

Finally, Jenner rocked a puffy pink satin jacket that added an organic shape to her silhouette and looked like a giant piece of bubblegum swirling around her body. And indeed, if anything, this look certainly gave us something to chew on.
