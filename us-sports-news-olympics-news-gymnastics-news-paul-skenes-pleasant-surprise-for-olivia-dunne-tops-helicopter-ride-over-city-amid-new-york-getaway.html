Paul Skenes Pleasant Surprise for Olivia Dunne Tops Helicopter Ride Over CityAmid New York

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

LSU gymnastics star Olivia Dunne and her boyfriend, minor league baseball pitcher Paul Skenes, delighted their followers with enchanting moments from their recent New York City date night on Instagram. With Dunne’s massive following of 4.5 million on the platform, they posted snapshots outside a helicopter and a captivating video showcasing the city skyline during their helicopter tour, mesmerizing as it was.

Recently, LSU’s top-earning female athlete took an unexpected turn by visiting New York City. Immersed in the vibrant spirit of the metropolis during her holiday, Dunne effortlessly shares her experiences. Now, she has brought some new surprises to the Big Apple, which has taken the city by storm.

Olivia Dunne: Balancing gymnastics, romance and canine love in NYC

Being a devoted dog mom presents its challenges. The responsibility of ensuring your furry companion feels loved and included spans every aspect of life, whether it’s a romantic date night, rigorous gymnastics training, a professional photo shoot, or even a relaxing holiday getaway. Olivia Dunne epitomizes this role flawlessly as she introduced her beloved canine companion, “Roux,” into her life in September. Recently, she delighted her Snapchat followers by sharing an endearing picture of her furry friend in the car, peering out the window as they ventured through the bustling streets of New York City. The adorable dog beautifully encapsulates the joy and warmth she undoubtedly brought along on her New York journey.

Dunne revealed on social media this past Tuesday that she and her boyfriend, Paul Skenes, treated themselves to an extravagant date night in New York City. She posted snapshots of them standing on a helipad with the captions ‘date night‘ and ‘Perfect,’ respectively. They also embraced as they took in views from atop Times Square, adding some romance for good measure.

Juggling gymnastics stardom and digital challenges

From her beginnings as a prodigious junior to becoming an LSU gymnastics star later, Olivia Dunne’s odyssey has been marked by trials and successes. Apart from her athletic career, the digital challenge she currently confronts is an asserted TikTok “shadow ban” that has resulted in a precipitous decline in interactions. Although the cause of this reversal is not clear, Dunne views setbacks with equanimity. She believes that what went wrong was a glitch on her platform; she only wants to learn how to handle social media stardom.

Blending gymnastics with the pressures of online fame, Dunne emphasizes the importance of balance. Recognizing the challenges of dealing with newfound fame, she resolves to maintain her peace of mind by compartmentalizing different aspects of her life. Fans eagerly anticipate her return to gymnastics and hope she swiftly navigates her way out of the online traps. Dunne’s humorous take on holiday gift troubles is a sweetly self-deprecating glimpse into life as a sensation in gymnastics circles.
