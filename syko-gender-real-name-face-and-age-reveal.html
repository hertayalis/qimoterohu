SYKO Gender And Wiki

As for musician SYKO's gender, it remains unknown.

Nonetheless, some reports do use the pronoun he/his. His face looks like that of a man. However, the artist himself hasn't disclosed anything about the subject. 

Likewise, SKYO's real name also remains a mystery at the moment. His official Twitter account, @lolsyko, says that he was born on 1 January 2001, meaning his age is 21. Likewise, it also shows that he is from Dallas, Texas.

The artist rose to prominence after releasing his highly-popular track, #BrooklynBloodPop!, in 2020. It became a trend on social media, including TikTok. Many people liked the catchy beat and the strangely nostalgic vibe of the early 2010-11 vibes it gave.

The song had over 27 million Spotify streams back then. Now, it has surpassed 150 million. Moreover, over 279.1 thousand videos are with #BrooklynBloodPop! on TikTok, showcasing the massive popularity of the track.

After it went viral, the major music labels called him. Further, he had talks with directors like JMP.  

SKYO's career in music began at 14 with EDM beats. He used to make them but stopped that journey and picked up vocals four years later on Christmas day. He believes that music is in his blood.

The musical artist's inspirations include the likes of Lady Gaga, Ke$ha, Britney, Chief Keef, XXX, Skrillex, Paramore, Saosin, and more. He also has a lot of rock/alt influences.
