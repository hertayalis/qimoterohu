Natasha Hamilton Husband: Meet Charles Gay

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 28, 2024

Natasha Hamilton Husband: Meet Charles Gay: Natasha Hamilton, officially known as Natasha Maria Hamilton is an English singer and a member of the girl group, Atomic Kitten.

She developed a love for music at a younger age and has been consistent throughout her career rising through the ranks to become one of the most sought-after artists.

In May 1999, Hamilton joined Atomic Kitten, a girl group founded by OMD frontman Andy McCluskey, replacing Heidi Range who left the early line-up.

In 2004, the group, Atomic Kitten announced they would be splitting to pursue solo careers and after barely a year, they reformed in 2005 and again in 2008.

In March 2012, Hamilton confirmed the trio would reform officially for the first time since 2004 to tour.

She has so far released 19 singles as a solo artist and three studio albums with the band including three number-one singles, their biggest being ‘Whole Again’ which was released in 2001.

In August 2015, Hamilton entered the Celebrity Big Brother house as a contestant and on 24 September reached the final on Day 29 and placed third.

In March 2023, Hamilton made headlines after she confirmed she has joined the Real Housewives of Cheshire cast. This comes after she was previously pictured with the show’s stars ahead of the new series launch later this month.

The Atomic Kitten star announced the news as she spoke of her new venture during an appearance on Friday’s This Morning hosted by Dermot O’Leary and Alison Hammond

Her announcement of joining the cast comes a few weeks after she disclosed that she was pregnant and expecting her fifth child with her husband, Charles Gay.

Natasha will join the glamorous line-up of ladies returning for series 16, including Seema Malhotra, Hanna Kinsella, Rachel Lugo, Lystra Adams, Sheena Lynch, Lauren Simon, and Nicole Sealey.

They’ll star alongside Guest Housewives Katie Alex, new girl Paige Chohan and beloved alum Tanya Bardsley.

Natasha Hamilton Husband: Meet Charles Gay

As of March 2023, Natasha Hamilton had been married twice. Her first marriage was to Riad Erraji. The pair got married on 23 November 2007 at Crewe Hall Hotel and separated in 2013.

After their split in 2013, Hamilton got engaged to Charles Gay in November 2014 and they eventually tied the knot on 25 September 2021 in Lake Como, Italy.

Charles lives his life away from the spotlight hence information regarding his date of birth, age, height, education, and occupation was not available at the time of this writing.
