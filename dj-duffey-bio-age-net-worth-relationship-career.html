Dj Duffey | Bio, Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Born and reared in Texas, DJ Duffey started her career as a rental car agent before rising to the top of the DJ world.

She performed at Amber Rose’s SlutWalk in 2015 and was a Currensy official DJ. The next year, she went on to star in Basketball Wives LA.

What is the Net Worth of DJ Duffey? Salary, Earnings

Various online sites estimate DJ Duffey’s net worth to be at $1 million. She also owns a Volkswagen that is worth $26,415 and is very proud of it. Duffey’s Instagram account had more than 1.2 million followers as of May 2022. Her account allows her to charge $5000 for a post.

Where was Dj Duffey born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, and Education

On August 25, 1988, DJ Duffey was born in Texas, in the United States. Her mother played collegiate basketball, while her father was in the military.

She is from a sports family—her older brother played collegiate football, and her younger brother got a scholarship to play quarterback at Texas Tech.

Duffy graduated from Mansfield Summit High School, where she played on the girls’ basketball team that won the state championship four times. Later, she spent four years playing Division 1 basketball at the University of Texas at Arlington.

Early in 2016 after their breakup, Duffey started dating NBA player Iman Shokuohizadeh. The two also got engaged in late 2016. Although they were in love, their relationship ended the following year.

How did DJ start her Professional Career?

When she had very little money in her account when she started her profession, Duffey worked for Enterprise Rent-A-Car. She later traveled to Dallas with her little son and eventually got in touch with Dallas DJ DJ ASAP.

After long days of work and being a full-time mom, Duffey started training for four to five hours every night and decided to dedicate the rest of her life to becoming a full-time DJ.

She eventually reached her goal of entertaining 300 people in one night. She quickly established a name for herself in Dallas and started to gain recognition on a larger scale.
