Kate Middleton Was Spotted Discount Shopping for Christmas, &amp; the Photos Are Perfect

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 5, 2024
  • Kate Middleton was spotted doing some last minute Christmas shopping at a discount store in the UK this week.
  • Photographed by fellow shoppers, the Duchess of Cambridge snapped up some goodies for the festive season, and was apparently accompanied by two of her children, Prince George and Princess Charlotte for the shopping trip.
  • According to The Sun, Kate was seen buying "trolley-loads of pictures, children’s books and artist materials," presumably to keep her three kids busy over the holiday season.

Kate Middleton surprised shoppers this week at British discount store The Range in King’s Lynn in Norfolk, which is close to the Sandringham Estate where the Cambridges are spending Christmas.

According to fellow shoppers, via The Sun, the duchess was shopping with two of her three children, with one source revealing, "I recognized little Prince George holding Kate’s hand and ­Charlotte with a red bow in her hair. Their protection officers were keeping a discreet distance so they looked like any other family out grabbing last-minute Christmas bargains."

According to Instagram user @middletonmaven, Kate’s tweed jacket is by Dubarry of Ireland. The sporty Dubarry Bracken coat is perfect for the winter season, and retails for around $600.

Previously, sources revealed to BAZAAR.com. that Kate would be spending Christmas Eve and Christmas Day with the royal family at Sandringham, along with Prince William and their three children. Sources also explained that Kate and William would be visiting the Middleton family at their home in Bucklebury, Berkshire, in the days following Christmas.
