Adele's Changing Looks

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 5, 2024

See Adele's beauty transformation through the years.

2008

At the Brit Awards, the songstress opted for her signature cat-eye liner-and won the Critics' Choice honor for her debut offering, 19.

2008

She accented her favorite asset with heavy fringe and dark liner. "I love my eyes, so I make them big," she told People.

2009

"When I'm working, I go for very dramatic makeup," Adele told InStyle of looks like this one. "I love contouring-Boy George style-with lots of blush on my cheeks."

2009

At VH1 Divas, she lived up to the title with a teased ponytail and '60s-inspired lashes.

2011

For the MTV Video Music Awards, the 21 singer went high volume with a major bouffant.

2012

She lightened up for the Grammys, courtesy of colorist Lucy Holbrook at London's Daniel Galvin salon. "We decided to color Adele's hair a honey-colored blond as it suited her complexion and really brought out the color of her eyes," Holbrook told People.com. "It was important to elect a color with plenty of depth to complement the bouncy voluminous styles that she favors."

2013

In keeping with her favored retro glam, Adele made her first post-baby appearance at the Golden Globes sporting a voluminous updo.

2013

The Grammy award winner opts for a braided 'do and dons her signature cat-eye.

2015

The singer's hair was voluminous, as usual, upon stepping out to sign autographs in New York.

2016

The songstress kept her hair soft and naturally textured while attending the 2016 Grammy Awards.

2017

The singer stunned at the Grammy Awards, rocking a tousled updo, her signature cat-eye, and a pale pink lip.
