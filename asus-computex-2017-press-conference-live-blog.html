ASUS Computex 2017 Press Conference Live Blog

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 28, 2024

01:45AM EDT - We are live from ASUS' press conference at Computex 2017. As usual, the company will announce a number of new products that it will start to sell in the coming months and quarters. Products announced by ASUS usually represent the industry's trends, so if you want to take a glimpse at the near future of personal computing, join in

02:00AM EDT - Nice name of the event, by the way.

02:01AM EDT - Welcome, Jonney Shih, chairman of ASUS

02:04AM EDT - Looks thin indeed. As always, made of metal.

02:06AM EDT - The convertible features a 13.3" 4K display.

02:06AM EDT - Supports all the latest Windows 10 features - Ink, Cortana, Modern Standby, etc.

02:07AM EDT - Fingerprint reader is on the edge of the display.

02:08AM EDT - Obviously, it has a very fast resume time thanks to Modern Standby.

02:09AM EDT - The ZenBook Flip S is based on Intel Core i7 with up to 3.5 GHz frequency. It also has 16 GB of RAM and up to 1 TB PCIe 3.0 x4 SSD. Neat.

02:09AM EDT - The ZenBook Flip S is based on Intel Core i7 with up to 3.5 GHz frequency. It also has 16 GB of RAM and up to 1 TB PCIe 3.0 x4 SSD. Neat.

02:09AM EDT - Battery capacity is 39 Wh, it lasts for 11.5 hours.

02:10AM EDT - Like other premium ZenBooks, it has an audio-sub-system co-developed with Harman.

02:11AM EDT - Now talking about 14"-class laptops.

02:11AM EDT - Now talking about 14"-class laptops.

02:12AM EDT - Apparently, announcing the all new 14" ASUS ZenBook 3 Deluxe.

02:12AM EDT - So, it is 12.9 mm thick and weighs 1.1 kilograms.

02:13AM EDT - Since this is a Deluxe, it is not only made of metal, but also comes with some gold elements.

02:14AM EDT - In addition, the ZenBook 3 Deluxe uses Corning Gorilla Glass 5 to protect the display.

02:15AM EDT - So, it has a Core i7 CPU, 16 GB of DDR4-2133 and up to 1TB PCIe 3.0 x4 SSD.

02:16AM EDT - As usual, Deluxe is not only about internals, but also about design. Besides, just like its predecessor, it has powerful speakers with Harman badge.

02:17AM EDT - Up next is... thye all new ASUS ZenBook Pro.

02:17AM EDT - So, it is beautiful and has all the business features you might need.

02:18AM EDT - Now, this one has a more powerful Core i7 H-series CPU, GeForce GTX 1050 Ti, up to 16 GB of DDR4-2400, up to 1 TB PCIe 3.0 x4 SSD and two TB3 ports.

02:19AM EDT - So, it is 18.9 mm thick and weighs 1.8 kilograms.

02:19AM EDT - It has a 15.6" 4K display.

02:20AM EDT - Due to the footprint, ASUS has managed to fit in a 79 Wh battery for a really long battery life.

02:22AM EDT - So, ASUS is announcing the new VivoBook S - a thin laptop that promises to be affordable too.

02:23AM EDT - "Empowered luxury for everyone to enjoy," - as Mr. Shih puts it.

02:24AM EDT - So, aluminum, 17.9 mm thick, weighs 1.5 kilograms.

02:25AM EDT - The display has 7.8 mm bezels, but no word about resolution.

02:26AM EDT - Nice to have a discrete GPU in an affordable laptop, but it will be interesting to see the exact benchmark numbers.

02:27AM EDT - Now, ASUS is announcing the VivoBook Pro - affordable business laptop series.

02:27AM EDT - This one has a Core i7 H CPU, GeForce GTX 1050, 16 GB of RAM, etc.
