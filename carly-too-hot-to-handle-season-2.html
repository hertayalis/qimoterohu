Carly Lawrence makes it clear from the beginning of Season 2 of 'Too Hot to Handle' that she's not looking for commitment. Here's what we know about her.

The first episodes of Season 2 of Too Hot to Handle are here, and things are already heating up on the beach. The cast of hot singles includes Carly Lawrence, who states from the get-go that she doesn't care about the $100,000 grand prize.
