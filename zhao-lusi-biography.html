Zhao Lusi Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 1, 2024

Who is Zhao Lusi?

Zhao Lusi is a well-known Chinese actress and singer with millions of social media followers. She is also known as Rosy and has been in films such as The Romance of Tiger and Rose (2020), Love Like the Galaxy (2022), and Dating in the Kitchen (2020).

Zhao Lusi was most recently seen in Hidden Love and GenZ in 2023. She is also hard at work on her next television shows, Zhu Lian Yu Mu and The Last Immortal.

Zhao Lusi: Bio, Age, Parents, Education, Ethnicity

Zhao Lusi, commonly known as Rosy, was born on November 9, 1998, in Chengdu, Sichuan, China. She will be 25 years old in 2023. She is of Chinese descent, although she has not revealed her ethnicity or the religion she practices.

There is no precise information provided concerning her personal matter, and her parents’ names and occupations have not been divulged. She has kept her personal life and her parents’ lives private from the media.

When it comes to her educational history, she hasn’t said much about it. She did, however, receive her degree from MingDao University. However, she has not said what major she graduated with.

Zhao Lusi: Profession, Career

Zhao Lusi debuted in the entertainment sector as the host of the variety show Huo Xing Qing Bao Ju for the Mars Intelligence Agency. Lusi made her acting debut in 2017 with a supporting part in the online drama Cinderella Chef. She appeared in the film City of Rock the same year.

Zhao rose to prominence in 2018 for her supporting part in the historical romance series Untouchable Lovers. She made her debut as a leading lady in the time-travel historical drama Oh! My Emperor the same year. Zhao’s fame grew as a result of the series’ success.

Zhao appeared in the 2019 romantic film Autumn Fairytale, which was based on the South Korean television serial Autumn in My Heart. She also appeared in the period fantasy Prodigy Healer and the historical romance Love Better Than Immortality in the same year. She went on to appear in the romantic drama I Hear You.
