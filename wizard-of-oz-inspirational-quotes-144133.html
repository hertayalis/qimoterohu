Wizard of Oz Inspirational Quotes Share on Pinterest, Facebook

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 5, 2024

We love revisiting our favorite old movies. They're always good for a little nostalgia, and some of them are all-time classics. The Wizard of Oz is one of those movies, and it feels like we're still learning new things about the flick even though it's been almost 80 years since the film's original release. But eight decades or not, this film stands up. In fact, it still inspires Halloween costumes and party themes to this day.

What we've really taken away from this film, though, is all the beautiful messages its characters learned on their journey. With Scarecrow looking for his brain, Tin Woodsman pining for a heart, Cowardly Lion seeking courage, and Dorothy wanting nothing more than to find her way back to her friends and family, this motley crew had a long journey before them. And as they made their way down the yellow brick road and to the Emerald City, they discovered the power of friendship, love, bravery, and wisdom.

And though we'll always love the quote, "There's no place like home," (because, truly, there is no place quite as good as home) there are plenty of wise words to live by in this film. They don't all come from the Wizard, either. Dorothy, Glinda, Scarecrow, Tin Man, and Cowardly Lion all have their share of lines and lessons we hold in our hearts. By the end of the flick, all these characters had found what they were looking for. And, as an audience, we'd also found some inspirational mottos and daily affirmations to live by.
